## Product Positioning: Vibe Design Tool

### Core Identity

**BananaTape is a "vibe design tool" — natural-language Photoshop for AI image models.**

Traditional design tools (Figma, Photoshop, Canva) assume the user understands composition, color theory, layers, masks, and design workflows. BananaTape removes that barrier entirely. If you can describe the vibe, paste a reference, and scribble a rough note on an image, you can create.

The brand metaphor is intentionally scrappy: banana-on-the-wall art meets duct-tape utility. It should feel playful, useful, and slightly hackerish — a tool that lets non-designers patch images into shape without learning professional design software.

### Target User

- **Non-designers** who need visuals but have zero design skills
- **Product managers / founders** iterating on app mockups, marketing assets, or social content
- **Developers** who need quick UI concepts or image edits without learning complex tools
- **Anyone** who thinks in words and rough sketches, not pixels and layers

### Why It Works

| Traditional Tool | BananaTape |
|---|---|
| Learn layers, masks, blending modes | Just type what you want |
| Master pen tools and bezier curves | Draw a rough box, arrow, or sticky note |
| Understand color palettes and typography | AI handles the visual execution |
| Navigate complex interfaces | Hand tool + zoom + generate |

### Key Differentiators

1. **Annotation-First Workflow**
   - Users don't "design" — they **annotate**.
   - Drop a memo on an image: *"Make this button blue"*, *"Add a cat here"*, *"Remove the background"*
   - The canvas is a conversation surface, not a pixel-precise artboard.

2. **Zero Learning Curve**
   - Default tool is the **hand** (pan), not a complex selection tool.
   - Generate → Annotate → Re-generate. That's the entire loop.
   - No layers panel. No mask modes. No export settings labyrinth.

3. **History as a Sidebar**
   - Every generation is saved automatically.
   - Non-designers don't think in "files" or "versions" — they think in *"the one I liked 3 tries ago."*
   - Click any history item to instantly restore that state.

### Future: Prompt Enhancement

**The biggest blocker for non-designers isn't the tool — it's knowing what to ask for.**

Future iterations will include **prompt enhancement**:

- **Smart Suggestions**: Based on the current image, suggest what to generate next (e.g., *"Try a darker mood"*, *"Add more contrast"*, *"Convert to illustration style"*)
- **Annotation-to-Prompt Translation**: Convert scribbled annotations into optimized AI prompts. A box around a person + memo *"remove"* becomes a precise inpainting prompt.
- **Template Prompts**: One-click prompts for common tasks — *"Make this look professional"*, *"Turn into a product mockup"*, *"Add shadow and depth"*
- **Style Memory**: Remember previously successful prompts and surface them when similar images are loaded.

The goal is to make the user feel like they have a **design-savvy assistant** built into the canvas — one that understands their vague scribbles and turns them into polished results.
